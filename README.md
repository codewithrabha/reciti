# ReCiti

**Your city, in your hands.**

ReCiti is a community app that turns everyday observations into real change. See a pothole, an overflowing bin, a broken streetlight — or a freshly cleaned park? Snap a photo, share it, and let your neighbours back you up and follow it through to a fix.

Together, we build an honest, living picture of how our city is really doing.

## How it works

**1. Spot it**
Notice something in your neighbourhood — good or bad. A problem that needs fixing, or a win worth celebrating.

**2. Capture it**
Take a quick photo, add a tag, and post it. Your report is automatically placed on the map, so others nearby know exactly where it is.

**3. Back it up**
Neighbours can verify your report to confirm it's real. The more people who stand behind it, the louder its voice.

**4. Follow it to the finish**
When something gets fixed, the person who reported it can share an "after" photo. The community confirms the fix — and everyone gets to celebrate the win.

## What you'll find inside

- **Pulse** — a simple dashboard showing how your city is doing right now: open issues, wins, and what still needs a hand.
- **Explore** — a live feed of what people are reporting around you.
- **Capture** — the heart of the app: report an issue or share a win in seconds.
- **Learn** — a daily civic-knowledge question to test what you know about your city and community.
- **Profile** — track your impact, climb the ranks, and see how you stack up on the community leaderboard.

## Earn your stripes

Every action you take helps your community — and earns you points along the way. Report an issue, verify a neighbour's post, share a fix, or answer the daily question. As your points grow, you rise through the ranks:

**Tourist → Resident → Advocate → Guardian**

The more you contribute, the more your city becomes truly *yours*.

## Why ReCiti?

Cities work best when the people who live in them are heard. ReCiti gives everyone a simple, powerful way to point at what matters, rally their neighbours, and watch real problems get solved — together.

Spot it. Share it. Fix it.
